Welcome to Berkeley's Fortune Finder Database

This database is designed to help you find your favorite fortunes no matter what they are. All of the fortunes that come standard, and many extra from plugins found with linux's apt-get have been organized into three convinient tables. Tables like Quotes, Q/A, and of course Fortunes. Find Quotes by Mark Twain, with or with out the profanity. Just sign in or sign up and enjoy your time at the top of the nav-bar.
